Our Journey

How we're revolutionizing education in Nigeria

The Vision Begins

SmartBooks was founded with a bold mission: to make digital learning accessible offline for every Nigerian student, regardless of internet access or location.

Early impact

We moved from idea to actiondesigning SmartBooks with direct input from teachers, parents, and students. Early prototypes were tested in a handful of classrooms, providing valuable feedback that shaped our content, user experience, and offline-first technology. These early trials confirmed a strong demand for accessible, digital learning without internet barriers.

Building Momentum

We completed development of the first version of SmartBooks and began piloting the platform with select schools. With support from educators, curriculum experts, and local partners, we refined the content and technology to ensure it meets the real needs of Nigerian classrooms.

The Future of Learning

SmartBooks is set to launch with a bold mission: to reach learners in all 36 states of Nigeria, combining offline access with the power of AI-driven, curriculum-aligned education. Our upcoming platform will feature: Interactive multimedia content Personalized voice tutoring Gamified learning experiences And a smart dashboard for parents and teachers Were building the future where every child can learn, grow, and succeedwithout needing internet access. SmartBooks isnt just a product. Its a movement to bridge the digital learning gap and reshape education across Nigeria.

Why We Started

In 2025, our founders - a team of educators and technologists - noticed Nigerian students struggling with:

  • Outdated physical textbooks
  • Poor internet access in rural areas
  • Lack of interactive learning tools

Our Solution

SmartBooks was built to solve these problems with:

  • 100% offline digital textbooks
  • Nigerian curriculum alignment
  • Gamified learning experiences
